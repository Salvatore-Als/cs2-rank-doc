Welcome to CS2-Rank

CS2-Rank is a powerful and community-driven Metamod plugin for Counter-Strike 2. It introduces a flexible ranking system that allows global and per-map ranks based on in-game events such as kills, deaths, suicides, headshots, etc.

The plugin is entirely configurable, giving you control over various aspects of the ranking system. It utilizes MySQL for data storage and retrieval.

To enhance your experience, we also provide a Discord bot and a web interface. These tools allow you to view the top players, check ranks, and perform other related actions.

Get ready to elevate your Counter-Strike 2 experience with CS2-Rank !
